How Reminders Work
  • Each benefit can have one or more reminder triggers (e.g., 7 days, 3 days, 1 day before period end).
  • An email is sent to each user's notification address when today matches a trigger and they still have unused credit on that benefit.
  • Users can turn reminders off entirely from their Preferences page.
  • Each trigger fires at most once per user per period.
