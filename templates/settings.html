How Reminders Work
  • Each benefit can have one or more reminder triggers (e.g., 7 days, 3 days, 1 day before period end).
  • An email is sent when today matches a trigger and the benefit still has unused credit.
  • Once the credit is fully used, no further reminders are sent for that period.
  • Each trigger fires at most once per period.
